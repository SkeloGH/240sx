Help Resources

Finding Help with your MegaSquirt:

An abundance of information has been compiled for MegaSquirt providing enough information to successfully get your vehicle going on EFI and running at its best. However, all this documentation can seem overwhelming to wade through. This list of resources and key understandings hopefully help you to get the answers you need. You will find it helpful to first understand the separate components to get to the help you need.

Firmware Manuals: 

The help files available here are specific to the use of TunerStudio. Setup and Tuning of the controller can be very specific to your Controller and Controllers firmware. For more information on the setup and tuning of your controller, check with the reseller or their web site for additional documentation specific to your controller.
