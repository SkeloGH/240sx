MS Extra Help Resources

Finding Help with your MegaSquirt:

An abundance of information has been compiled for MegaSquirt providing enough information to successfully get your vehicle going on EFI and running at its best. However, all this documentation can seem overwhelming to wade through. This list of resources and key understandings hopefully help you to get the answers you need. You will find it helpful to first understand the separate components to get to the help you need. Beyond the help offered here these addtional links will provide information on setting up and configuring your Controller.

MS Extra Firmware: 

The developers of MSEXTRA have invested countless hours developing, documenting and supporting the Megasquirt firmwares to deliver the features users are requesting. The Firmware developers work is essential to many TunerStudio features along with meu navigations and dialog definitions.

MS Extra Firmware Manuals: 

As TunerStudio and Megatune use the same ECU Definition ("ini") files for MS1 & MS2, the Navigation and dialog layouts are the same and the same manuals are valid for TunerStudio. Some older firmwares have references to MegaTune configuration, disregard these sections, but the settings for the firmware will be the same.
